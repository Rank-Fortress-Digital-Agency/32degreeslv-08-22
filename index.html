Check for licensing and insurance.


The first thing you’ll want to do is check to see if the heating service is licensed and insured. This will protect you in case something goes wrong during your work. If the heating service is not licensed or insured, you could be held responsible for any damages that occur. Make sure to ask for this information before you hire anyone.

The next thing you’ll want to do is make sure that the heating contractor you’re considering is experienced in working on your type of system. You don’t want to hire someone who’s never worked on a gas furnace before. Ask for references from previous customers and check out their online reviews.

Finally, you’ll want to get an estimate of how much it will cost to have the work done. Be sure to get at least three different estimates so that you can compare prices. Don’t automatically go with the lowest estimate, however. Be sure to ask about any hidden costs or fees that could be associated with the job.
